Design Decisions

Every Detail
Has a Reason

Premium Imagery Only

Real GL Homes photography. No stock photos of strangers. The home is the hero — always.

CTA Hierarchy

Navy = primary action. Blue = explore. Ghost = secondary. Never two primary CTAs side by side.

3-Field Form

First name · Last name · Email. Every extra field costs 11% conversion. Keep it minimal, earn more leads.

Trust Signals

40+ years · 100,000+ homeowners · Privacy badge. Credibility before the ask.
